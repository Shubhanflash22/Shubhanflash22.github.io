Autonomous Roadside Mechanic

open_in_new

ROS2 • Jetson Nano • YOLO • LiDAR

Engineered a distributed multi-agent robotic system to autonomously identify and assist disabled vehicles. A Jetson Nano acts as a "bird's-eye" scout, using OpenCV and FFT-based signal processing to detect hazard lights and dispatch a ground robot. The robot leverages sensor fusion (OAK-D camera + LiDAR) and a custom YOLO model to navigate through traffic and park precisely behind the target vehicle with 90% detection accuracy.

Renewable Storage Optimization

open_in_new

LSTM • ANN • PyTorch • VRFB

Developed a Deep Learning framework to optimize the efficiency of Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries (VRFB). By modeling complex non-linear relationships between electrolyte flow rates and state-of-charge using ANN, LSTM, and RNN architectures, I achieved a 40–70% improvement in power loss prediction accuracy over baseline methods, directly enabling more cost-effective renewable energy storage operations.
